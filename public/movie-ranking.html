The Best Movies According to Vikram

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Amazing Spider-Man

This is my favourite movie, absolutely love how Andrew Garfield adapts to the character of spider-man and the action scenes with the quality of Sony is just majestic. Also love the story of the movie.

Baby Driver

Love the plot of the movie and the cinematics of the movie are amazing.

Thor Ragnarok

A movie with perfect cinematography, and the story of an overpowered villian's fall is just mind blowing.
